FAQs

Does it work with any retail product?

It supports common retail barcodes (EAN/UPC) and works best with clear, well‑lit product front label photos.

Why does the barcode scan sometimes fail or show the wrong product?

Barcodes are not always reliable identifiers on the internet. Many products either do not appear in public barcode databases or are listed incorrectly. In some cases, barcode numbers are reused across different products. Put simply: bad data leads to bad AI.
That’s why the option to scan the front label of the product with your camera is provided, which gives the AI a much more accurate way to recognize the product.

Does it require an account?

No account is required to start scanning.
